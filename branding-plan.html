08 ‚Äî Launch Playbook

Making the Entrance

Pre-Launch (2-4 weeks before)

1. Lock the Name & Domain
Secure domain, all social handles, and App Store developer name. Register them ALL the same day to prevent squatting.
2. Teaser Content
Start posting educational astrocartography content from the brand account. Build audience before the app even launches. "Something is coming" is overplayed ‚Äî provide value immediately.
3. Beta With Creators
Send beta access to 10-20 astrology micro-influencers (5K-50K followers). Let them play with their own map. Ask for honest reactions, not scripted promos.
4. Helena Woods Outreach
Reach out to Helena personally. Offer her a featured astrocartographer role, or a "Helena's Picks" section. She's the gateway to the entire astrocartography community.

Launch Week

5. "Your Map" Campaign
Invite users to share screenshots of their astrocartography map. Make the map visually beautiful enough that people WANT to share it. This is your viral loop.
6. ProductHunt / Hacker News
Launch on Product Hunt for tech-curious early adopters. Position as "the Figma of astrocartography" ‚Äî beautiful design meets complex data.
7. Reddit r/astrology
Genuine participation (not spam). Answer astrocartography questions. Share the app when relevant. This community is hungry for good astrocartography tools.
8. App Store Optimization
Keywords: "astrocartography," "astro map," "birth chart map," "travel astrology," "relocation astrology." Beautiful screenshots showing the actual map experience.

Post-Launch Growth

Social Cause Integration
CHANI donates 5% to FreeFrom. Consider: donating a percentage to a travel/displacement cause. Values-driven branding creates loyalty with your demographic.
Community Building
Build around relocation stories and digital nomad lifestyle. Discord or community tab. Let users share their experiences on their planetary lines.
Professional Tier
Add paran lines and advanced features. Become the tool professional astrocartographers recommend to clients. Helena Woods' certification graduates need tools.
Travel Brand Partnerships
Pitch Airbnb, Moxy Hotels, travel brands on "astrology travel guides." Sanctuary already proved this works. You have the better product for it.

Astrocartography is trending but no one owns it yet. Co-Star, CHANI, and The Pattern haven't touched location astrology. You have a 12-18 month window to become THE astrocartography app.
